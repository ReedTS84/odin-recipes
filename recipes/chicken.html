Chicken Marsala

Fast Easy Chicken Marsala

If you're looking for ways to make chicken a little less boring, put this recipe in your pocket. The classic creamy, savory sauce is easy to make and so good you'll want to drizzle it over everything.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup All-Purose Flour
  • 8 chicken cutlets or 4 chicken breasts, sliced in half (about 1.5 lbs)
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. canola oil
  • 2 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 (8-ounce) package sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 c. chopped onion (about 1/2 small onion)
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 c. dry Marsala wine
  • 3/4 c. chicken stock
  • 1/2 c. heavy cream
  • 1 tsp. fresh thyme, plus more for garnish
